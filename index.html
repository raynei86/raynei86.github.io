The modern web sucks

There is a website bloat crisis. Bloated sites are full of ads, scripts, and trackers.

The number of bloated sites have grown so much that people have become numb to them. While they try to do simple browsing, they tolerate having an advertisement autoplay, cookies tracking them in the background, and useless scripts slowing down the page

This website is meant to suck less. There are no ads, no trackers, and no scripts; just a site you can sanely browse.
